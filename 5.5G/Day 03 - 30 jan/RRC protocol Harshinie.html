Select a component on the left to view architectural details. The RRC (Radio Resource Control) acts as the central controller of the Access Stratum.

State Management Model

Energy Saving

RRC_IDLE

Minimal power consumption. UE monitors paging and performs cell reselection. No connection exists at the gNB level.

5G Innovation

RRC_INACTIVE

The "Suspended" state. The UE and gNB keep the AS Context saved. Allows near-instant transition to Connected with zero signaling overhead.

High Throughput

RRC_CONNECTED

Active data transmission. The UE has a dedicated C-RNTI and follows network-controlled handovers.

Core Procedures

System Information

Handles MIB and SIBs. 5G NR uses "On-Demand" SIB delivery to save radio resources.

Paging

Used to reach UEs in Idle or Inactive states for incoming calls or system info updates.

Measurement Reporting

RRC configures the UE to report signal quality (RSRP/RSRQ) to facilitate seamless handovers.
