UE

RRC State Machine

RRC_IDLE UE sleeps, monitors paging. No gNB context. Battery saving max.
RRC_INACTIVE (5G New!) Context saved in gNB. Fast resume, low latency, medium battery.
RRC_CONNECTED Active data transfer. UE context in both gNB and AMF.
Exam Highlight: RRC_INACTIVE is the "magic" of 5G. It allows the UE to stay in a "ready" state without the heavy signaling overhead of full re-connection.
Handover (Xn-Based)

Seamless Mobility

Measurement Report: UE detects that gNB_Beta signal strength (RSRP) is higher than gNB_Alpha.
Handover Request: Source gNB sends context to Target gNB via the Xn Interface.
Reconfiguration: UE switches frequency and synchronizes with gNB_Beta.

This process happens in less than 50ms to ensure you don't drop your VoIP call or gaming session.
