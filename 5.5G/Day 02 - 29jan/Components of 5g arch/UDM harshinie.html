Unified Data Management

Strictly following 3GPP TS 23.501, the UDM provides services for subscriber data management, authentication, and mobility management. It acts as the central "Brain" for identity.

Standard Interfaces

• N8: Facilitates AMF registration and location tracking.
• N10: Provides SMF with user session profiles.
• N13: Delivers authentication vectors to the AUSF.

Cloud-Native UDR

The 5G UDM is stateless. It retrieves all persistent data from the UDR (Unified Data Repository) over the Nudr interface, ensuring high availability.
