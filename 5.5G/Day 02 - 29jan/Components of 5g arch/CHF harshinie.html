Charging Function (CHF)

The CHF is the heart of the 5G Converged Charging System. It manages both Event-based and Session-based charging.

In 5G, charging is a service. Other Network Functions (NFs) act as "Charging Triggers" and consume the CHF service.

Click the CHF node to see the charging interaction web.

Nchf Interface

The service-based interface used by SMF and AMF to report usage and request quotas.

Converged Charging

Unlike 4G, CHF handles Online (pre-paid) and Offline (post-paid) charging simultaneously in a single flow.

Quota Management

For pre-paid users, CHF grants "Quotas" (e.g., 100MB). Once used, the SMF must ask CHF for more or cut the session.
